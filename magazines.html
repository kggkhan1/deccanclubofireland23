Submit Your Work

We welcome submissions from young writers, artists, and photographers in our community.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Open to community members aged 10-25
  • Accepted formats: articles, poetry, short stories, artwork, photography
  • Word limit: 1000 words for articles, 40 lines for poetry
  • Images should be high resolution (300dpi)
  • Email submissions to: youthmagazine@deccanclub.ie
  • Deadline for next issue: 15th of each quarter
